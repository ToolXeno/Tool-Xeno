What is Glass Skin?

Key Characteristics

  • Poreless-looking appearance with minimal visible pores
  • Smooth, even texture without roughness or bumps
  • High hydration levels creating plump, healthy skin
  • Natural luminosity and light-reflecting properties
  • Clear, translucent appearance without blemishes

Assessment Factors

  • Surface texture and smoothness evaluation
  • Hydration and moisture barrier function
  • Natural glow and light reflection capacity
  • Overall skin clarity and transparency
