Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, Tool Xeno's OCR tool is completely free for both personal and professional use. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions required.

How accurate is the text extraction?

We use a professional-grade OCR engine that provides up to 99.8% accuracy, especially with high-resolution images. The accuracy depends on image quality, font clarity, and contrast.

What languages are supported?

We support over 25 major languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Arabic, Russian, and Portuguese.

Do you store my images?

No. Your privacy is our priority. Images are processed securely via the OCR engine and are immediately deleted after processing. We do not store or share your uploaded files.

What file formats can I upload?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and WebP. For best results, use clear images with a resolution of at least 300 DPI.
