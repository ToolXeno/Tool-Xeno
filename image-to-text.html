Frequently Asked Questions

What is OCR and how does it work?

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) is a technology that converts different types of documents, such as scanned paper documents, PDF files, or images captured by a digital camera, into editable and searchable data. Our tool uses Tesseract.js, an advanced AI-powered OCR engine.

What file formats are supported?

We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TIFF, and WebP image formats. Maximum file size is 10MB for optimal performance.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes! All OCR processing happens directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Which languages are supported?

Our tool supports 100+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, Thai, Vietnamese, and many more.
