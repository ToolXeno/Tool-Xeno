Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our absolute priority. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: August 22, 2025

Welcome to Tool Xeno ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our principles and practices regarding data, especially highlighting that our tools are designed to be completely client-side.

1. The Core Principle: Your Data is Your Own

Tool Xeno operates on a fundamental principle of privacy: your data never leaves your device. The vast majority of our tools (e.g., image converters, document compressors, text analyzers) perform all their processing directly in your web browser. This means the files, text, and other data you work with are never uploaded to our servers. We have no access to them, and we do not store them.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Because our tools are client-side, we do not collect, see, or store any personal or sensitive information you process using them. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • The content of your documents, images, or text.
  • Any personal information contained within your files.
  • Data generated by the tools (e.g., converted files, compressed images).

3. Information We Collect (Limited)

While using our website, we may collect anonymous, non-personal information to improve our service:

  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous data about website traffic, such as which tools are most popular and how users navigate the site. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about the device and browser you are using (e.g., browser type, operating system) to ensure our site functions correctly on different platforms.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use `localStorage` for one specific purpose: to save your theme preference (light or dark mode). This is stored in your browser and is not a tracking cookie. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services, such as web font providers (Google Fonts) and content delivery networks (CDNs). These services may collect their own anonymous data as per their respective privacy policies. We do not share any personal information with them.

6. Data Security

The best security is not having data to protect. Since we do not store your files or personal data from our tools, the risk of a data breach involving your content is eliminated on our end. All processing is handled securely within your browser's sandboxed environment.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at: toolture@gmail.com.
